Applications

IRP uses structured applications to maintain serious roleplay standards. Applications are designed to evaluate mindset, maturity, and roleplay decision-making.

How it works: Fill out the questionnaire, then submit it through Discord support. This keeps everything in one place for staff review.

What we look for

Scene integrityYou stay in character, respect outcomes, and use tickets after scenes.
MaturityYou communicate calmly, avoid drama escalation, and accept staff decisions.
Roleplay depthYou build character goals and relationships instead of chasing quick wins.
AccountabilityYou can admit mistakes, learn, and improve without ego.

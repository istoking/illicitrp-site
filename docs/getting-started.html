Getting Started

This guide helps you join IRP and get into the city with minimum friction.

Before you join

  • Read the SOP Library and the Reporting Guide.
  • Set your voice and keybinds so you can communicate clearly in scenes.
  • Have a basic character concept: background, goals, personality, and boundaries.

First day checklist

Choose a character directionPick a believable identity and a simple starting goal.
Learn the city pacingDo not rush to big crimes. Build relationships and reputation.
Respect scene flowStay IC and handle disputes after the scene with a ticket.
Use the ticket systemWhen something is wrong, finish the scene and report properly.

Need help

If you get stuck, open a Discord ticket. Staff will help you calmly and professionally.

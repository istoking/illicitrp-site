FAQ

How do I report rule breaks

Stay in character, finish the scene, and submit a ticket with clear evidence. Use the Reporting Guide.

What is serious RP

Serious roleplay focuses on believable behavior, consistent character choices, realistic pacing, and respecting outcomes. Winning is not the goal. Story is the goal.

Can I join a gang

Yes. Recruitment and expectations are covered in the Gang SOP. Gangs require maturity, consistent presence, and clean conduct.

What if a system bugs mid-scene

Do not break character. Finish the scene in a reasonable way, then open a ticket with details and clips if possible.

Do you allow random killing or vehicle ramming

No. No random deathmatch and no vehicle deathmatch. Conflict must be built through roleplay and escalation standards.

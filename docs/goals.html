Goals and Community Direction

IRP is built for longevity. We aim to create a city where players can invest in stories that continue for months, not minutes.

Short-term goals

  • Improve onboarding and player guidance across all roles.
  • Consistency in enforcement and ticket outcomes.
  • Polish economy and progression to reward good roleplay.

Long-term goals

  • Expand faction depth for gangs, businesses, and government roles.
  • Build a living city with seasonal events and evolving story arcs.
  • Maintain production-level performance as content grows.

Our passions

ImmersionRoleplay that feels grounded, paced, and believable.
FairnessClear standards and consistent enforcement for everyone.
CraftWell-built systems that enhance RP instead of replacing it.

About Illicit Roleplay

Illicit Roleplay (IRP) is a serious roleplay community built around long-term character development, immersive scenes, and consistent enforcement. We focus on strong storytelling, respectful player conduct, and roleplay outcomes that feel earned.

Our mission: create an immersive city where choices matter, actions have consequences, and every player has room to build a meaningful story.

What we value

  • Scene integrity: stay in character, respect outcomes, and let moments breathe.
  • Respect: firm rules, calm conversations, and zero drama escalation.
  • Consistency: clear SOPs, clear expectations, and fair enforcement.
  • Quality: optimized systems, stable resources, and professional operations.

Server identity

Serious RP Character-first roleplay, realistic pacing, meaningful stakes.
Rules enforced Clear standards, consistent tickets, and professional moderation.
Performance focused Low-impact resources, optimized UI, and production-grade scripts.

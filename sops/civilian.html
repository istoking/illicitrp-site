IRP Civilian SOP

Production-level SOP for IRP. For the full document, download the PDF/DOCX below.

Quick Standard Summary

  • Stay IC in city and during scenes.
  • No OOC arguments mid-scene. Report after.
  • Scene integrity: no combat logging or stalling.
  • Escalation and Fear RP standards.
  • Evidence-based ticket reporting.
Golden Rule: Finish the scene IC. If something is wrong, report after via Discord tickets with evidence.

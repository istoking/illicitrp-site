IRP Gang SOP

Production-level SOP for IRP. For the full document, download the PDF/DOCX below.

Quick Standard Summary

  • Official gangs: Lost MC, Ballas, Vagos, Cartel, Families, Triads.
  • No instant KOS. Escalation and lead-up required.
  • No grief loops. Violence must create follow-up RP.
  • Hostage, kidnap, robbery standards.
  • Respect LEO and EMS as RP partners.
Golden Rule: Finish the scene IC. If something is wrong, report after via Discord tickets with evidence.

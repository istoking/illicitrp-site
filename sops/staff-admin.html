IRP Staff & Admin SOP

Production-level SOP for IRP. For the full document, download the PDF/DOCX below.

Quick Standard Summary

  • Neutrality, consistency, evidence-based decisions.
  • Production ticket workflow and documentation.
  • Graduated discipline with severity overrides.
  • Minimal live intervention: preserve RP first.
  • Discord operations, reimbursements, and events.
Golden Rule: Finish the scene IC. If something is wrong, report after via Discord tickets with evidence.

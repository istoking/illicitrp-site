IRP LEO & EMS SOP

Production-level SOP for IRP. For the full document, download the PDF/DOCX below.

Quick Standard Summary

  • Professionalism and calm tone are mandatory.
  • Scene-first decisions: do not rush outcomes.
  • Proportional escalation and force.
  • Investigation standards: evidence and follow-ups.
  • EMS neutrality and scene safety.
Golden Rule: Finish the scene IC. If something is wrong, report after via Discord tickets with evidence.

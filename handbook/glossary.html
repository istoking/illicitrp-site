Glossary

Common IRP terms used across SOPs.

Roleplay Terms

  • IC: In Character.
  • OOC: Out of Character.
  • Scene: A continuous RP interaction (stop, robbery, chase, medical, investigation).

Rule Concepts

  • RDM: Random Deathmatch.
  • VDM: Vehicle Deathmatch.
  • Powergaming: Forcing outcomes or unrealistic advantage play.
  • Metagaming: Using OOC information IC.
  • FailRP: Breaking realism or ignoring consequences.
  • NVL: Not valuing life when faced with credible threat.

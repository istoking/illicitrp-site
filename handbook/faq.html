FAQ

What if someone is breaking rules in my scene?

Finish the scene IC. Do not accuse mid-scene. Open a ticket after with evidence.

Can staff fix things mid-scene?

Staff avoids interrupting RP. They intervene immediately only for severe harassment, clear cheating harming multiple players right now, or server stability issues.

Do I need clips to report?

Clips are strongly recommended for consistent outcomes. Screenshots help but rarely show full context.

Where do I download SOPs?

Go to Downloads or each SOP page for PDF and DOCX.

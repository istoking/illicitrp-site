Reporting Handbook

This guide explains how to report issues in a NoPixel-style way: clean, evidence-based, and after the scene.

When to Report

  • Cheating, exploits, or clear mechanics abuse impacting others.
  • Powergaming, metagaming, FailRP, harassment, or hate speech.
  • Bug abuse affecting scenes or the economy.

When Not to Interrupt a Scene

  • Most rule concerns: finish the scene IC, then ticket after.
  • Do not accuse mid-scene or escalate OOC on voice.
Immediate Staff Intervention Only: severe harassment/hate speech, clear cheating harming multiple players right now, or server stability issues.

Evidence Standard

  • Clips should include context before the issue and the outcome.
  • Do not edit clips in a misleading way; staff may request full footage.
  • Keep reports factual. Staff will determine outcomes.

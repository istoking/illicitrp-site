Operating Standards

IRP follows a production-style standard: keep scenes immersive, prioritize story over mechanics, and handle issues calmly through tickets after scenes conclude.

Golden Rule: Do not argue OOC inside active RP. Finish the scene IC, then report concerns with evidence.
